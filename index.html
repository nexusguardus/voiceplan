FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers

Is VoicePlan HIPAA compliant?
Yes — end-to-end encryption, no PHI used in model training, no PHI stored beyond the duration of a call, secure data centers. A Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is available on request for all plans. VoicePlan is built to HIPAA standards from the ground up, not bolted on.
How long does setup take?
Most practices are live within 30 minutes of booking a demo. We handle script customization and staging. You simply point your phone number to us and approve the greeting script. Live test calls with our team are included — no IT work required on your side.
Will patients know they're talking to an AI?
No — VoicePlan uses advanced LLM voice technology with zero robotic characteristics. Patients consistently report believing they're speaking with a human front desk staff member. We recommend disclosing AI assistance only on first call or in-office signage if you prefer transparency.
Which practice management systems do you integrate with?
Native integrations for Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, and most major healthcare PMS platforms. Calendar sync with Google Calendar, Calendly, and Outlook. We can build custom integrations via Zapier or API for most other systems — reached our team to confirm fit.
What happens if VoicePlan can't handle a call?
Every plan includes configurable escalation rules — complex insurance questions, medical emergencies, or specific patient types can be routed live to your on-call staff via SMS alert, call transfer, or pager. You are always in the loop.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no long-term contracts, no early termination fees. Cancel in one click from your dashboard and we port your number back to you on the effective date of cancellation. Prorated billing for partial months applies where legally required.
