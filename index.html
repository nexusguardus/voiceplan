    ⚠️ Warnings & Best Practices 2026
    
      
    Hard-won lessons — don't get caught by surprise.
    
      
    
        
  • ✅ Set billing alerts at 50/75/90/100% on every cloud account before you get a bill shock.
    • 
        
  • 📅 Always Free ≠ forever — limits get updated. Oracle ARM VMs still holding strong in May 2026.
    • 
        
  • ⏰ Free trial ≠ Always Free — promotional credits (AWS $200, Azure $200) expire; build a plan within the window.
    • 
        
  • 📝 Track every account + payment method in a password manager — overlapping signups nullify start-up credits.
    • 
        
  • 🏢 Free tier ≠ commercial use — read the ToS. AWS Activate/Payless open-sourced but commercial use restrictions vary.
    • 
        
  • ⚠️ Groq compound tools are now paid — web_search, code_interpreter, browser.open cost money; factor that in.
    • 
        
  • 💳 ElevenLabs switched to credits — 10K credits/mo in 2026 (was character-based in 2024/25).
    • 
        
  • 🦅 AWS Free Tier $200 message — signs up now shading "$200". Underlying limits unchanged; confirm on signup page.
    • 
        
  • 🌏 OpenAI regional blocks — China, Hong Kong partial, Russia, Korea blocked in 2026. India beta depends on card currency.
    • 
        
  • 🔐 HIPAA BAA — if you're in healthcare: Anthropic (Claude), Google Cloud, AWS all have HIPAA BAA programs. Groq/ElevenLabs do not (as of May 2026).
